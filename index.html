Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and institute

What courses are available at Gurukul Technical Institute?
We offer 147+ skill-based and career-oriented courses including Computer Applications, Digital Marketing, Web Development, Software Development (C, C++, Java, Python), Data Science, Cyber Security, Graphic Design, Video Editing, Accounting, Tally, GST, Database Management (SQL), PHP, and many more specialized programs.
Is Gurukul Technical Institute NIELIT affiliated?
Yes, Gurukul Technical Institute is officially affiliated with NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology) under the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of India. This ensures our courses meet national standards and are recognized across the industry.
Are practical classes provided?
Absolutely! We emphasize hands-on practical training with state-of-the-art computer labs. Students work on live projects and real-world assignments to gain industry-ready skills. Our learning approach focuses on 70% practical and 30% theoretical education.
Is placement support available?
Yes, we provide comprehensive career support including resume building, portfolio development, interview preparation, and career guidance. While we don't guarantee placements, we prepare students thoroughly and connect them with industry opportunities to help launch their IT careers successfully.
Are beginners welcome?
Yes, absolutely! We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Our courses are designed to accommodate beginners with no prior experience as well as professionals looking to upgrade their skills. Our expert trainers ensure personalized attention in small batch sizes.
