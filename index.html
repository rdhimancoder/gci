Our Story & Mission

20 Years of Shaping
Skilled Professionals

Established in 2003 at Subhash Chowk, Sonipat, Gurukul Technical Institute has been the cornerstone of quality technical education in Haryana. Our institute was founded on the philosophy that every student deserves access to industry-relevant, hands-on training that directly translates to career opportunities.

As a proud affiliate of NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology, Ministry of Electronics & IT, Govt. of India), and recognized by HKCL (Government of Haryana), our certificates carry national credibility and industry trust. Every course we offer combines theoretical foundations with extensive practical lab sessions led by working industry professionals.

Our signature approach — small batch sizes, expert mentors, real-world project portfolios, and post-training placement support — has helped thousands of students secure employment in IT firms, digital agencies, government departments, and entrepreneurial ventures across India.

Our Journey Timeline
2003 — Foundation Year
Gurukul Technical Institute founded with a vision to bridge the digital skill gap in Sonipat, Haryana with first 5 computer courses.
2008 — NIELIT Affiliation Secured
Obtained prestigious national affiliation with NIELIT, Ministry of Electronics & IT, Govt. of India — a milestone ensuring quality standards.
2015 — Infrastructure Expansion
New smart classrooms, dedicated software labs, and multimedia stations established. Course catalogue expanded to 80+ programs.

2024 — 147+ Courses & Counting
Now offering 147+ skill-based courses across technology, design, accounting, and vocational trades, serving thousands yearly.
